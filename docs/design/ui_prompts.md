# UI Design Themes & Prompts

This document outlines four distinct UI design themes for a "healing" and "fresh" mood tracker/journaling app. Each theme includes a visual description and the specific prompt used to generate concept art.

## 1. Matcha Latte (Nature & Calm)
**Concept**: Emulating the natural, breathing feel of nature. Best for daily journaling and mood tracking.
*   **Keywords**: Healing, Airy, Natural, Soft.
*   **Color Palette**: Creamy White (Background), Sage Green (Primary), Oatmeal (Secondary).
*   **Prompt**:
    > Mobile app UI design, mood tracker, healing aesthetic, soft creamy white background with sage green and oatmeal accents, rounded cards, playful icons, soft shadows, cozy atmosphere, matcha latte color palette, high fidelity, simplistic, dribbble style.

## 2. Taro Cloud (Dreamy & Night)
**Concept**: A serene, almost magical vibes for evening use or deep reflection.
*   **Keywords**: Dreamy, Quiet, Glassmorphism, Moon.
*   **Color Palette**: Pale Grey-Blue (Background), Lavender (Primary), Baby Blue (Secondary).
*   **Prompt**:
    > Mobile app UI design, journaling app, dreamy aesthetic, soft grey-blue background with lavender and baby blue accents, glassmorphism cards, moon and star icons, soothing atmosphere, taro milk tea color palette, high fidelity, simplistic, dribbble style.

## 3. Peach Oolong (Warm & Energetic)
**Concept**: A vibrant yet soft theme that encourages activity and positivity.
*   **Keywords**: Sunny, Warm, Fruity, Energetic.
*   **Color Palette**: Soft Pink-Orange (Background), Apricot (Primary), Tender Green (Accents).
*   **Prompt**:
    > Mobile app UI design, habit tracker, warm aesthetic, soft pink-orange background with apricot and tender green accents, rounded buttons, fruit icons, sunny atmosphere, peach oolong color palette, high fidelity, simplistic, dribbble style.

## 4. Ocean Breeze (Fresh & Focus)
**Concept**: A clean, clarifying theme for meditation, focus modes, or clearing the mind.
*   **Keywords**: Fresh, Clean, Airy, Flowing.
*   **Color Palette**: Pale Cyan (Background), Seafoam (Primary), White (Accents).
*   **Prompt**:
    > Mobile app UI design, meditation or focus app, fresh aesthetic, pale cyan background with seafoam and white accents, clean lines, water droplet or breeze icons, airy atmosphere, ocean breeze color palette, high fidelity, simplistic, dribbble style.
